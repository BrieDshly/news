Seoul Flame BBQ

Posted on February 11, 2026 | By Admin | Category: New Restaurant In Town

At Seoul Flame BBQ, dining is more than just a meal, it’s an interactive culinary experience. Specializing in authentic Korean barbecue, the restaurant invites guests to gather around built-in tabletop grills where premium cuts of marinated meats are cooked fresh right before their eyes. The lively sound of sizzling grills and the aroma of savory marinades create an atmosphere that’s both exciting and welcoming.

Our menu features a wide selection of high-quality meats, including tender beef bulgogi, flavorful spicy pork, juicy galbi (short ribs), and garlic-marinated chicken. Each cut is carefully prepared using traditional Korean recipes, blending soy sauce, sesame oil, fresh garlic, and signature house spices to create bold, balanced flavors.

Every barbecue order is served with crisp lettuce wraps, steamed rice, and a colorful assortment of banchan(traditional Korean side dishes) that may include kimchi, seasoned vegetables, pickled radish, and savory pancakes. Guests can customize each bite by combining grilled meat with fresh vegetables and house-made dipping sauces like ssamjang and sesame oil salt.

Beyond the grill, Seoul Flame BBQ also offers classic Korean favorites such as hearty bibimbap, comforting kimchi stew, and savory Korean fried rice, making it perfect for both barbecue enthusiasts and first-time diners.

With its modern yet cozy décor, upbeat music, and friendly staff ready to guide guests through the grilling process, Seoul Flame BBQ is ideal for family dinners, group celebrations, or casual nights out with friends.

Seoul Flame BBQ — where flavor meets fire, and every meal becomes a shared experience.

Popular Menus

    Signature Beef Bulgogi Platter

    Our best-selling classic features thinly sliced premium beef marinated in a savory blend of soy sauce, sesame oil, fresh garlic, pear puree, and house spices. Grilled to perfection right at your table, the beef becomes tender, juicy, and slightly caramelized for a rich, smoky flavor. Served with crisp lettuce wraps, steamed rice, ssamjang dipping sauce, and a full selection of traditional banchan for a perfectly balanced bite every time.

    Spicy Galbi Short Ribs

    A customer favorite for bold flavor lovers, our bone-in short ribs are marinated in a sweet and spicy gochujang-based sauce infused with garlic, ginger, and chili flakes. As they sizzle on the grill, the marinade caramelizes beautifully, creating a smoky, slightly charred finish. Enjoy them wrapped in fresh lettuce with pickled radish, kimchi, and a drizzle of sesame oil for a fiery and satisfying barbecue experience.

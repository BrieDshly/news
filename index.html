Seoul Flame BBQ

Seoul Flame BBQ is a Korean Barbecue restaurant offering premium marinated meats grilled right at the table. Guests can enjoy classic cuts like beef bulgogi, spicy pork, and short ribs, served with fresh lettuce wraps, flavorful dipping sauces, and a variety of traditional side dishes (banchan). With its lively atmosphere and authentic flavors, Seoul Flame BBQ delivers a fun and satisfying dining expereince.

    Restaurant of the Week

    Golden Claypot

    Golden Claypot restaurant specializing in traditional claypot dishes. The menu features rice and noodle claypots topped with savory meats, fresh vegetables, and rich, aromatic sauces cooked and served hot in claypots. Known for its comforting flavors and homestyle feel, Golden Claypot offers a warm dining experience perfect for satisfying, hearty meals.

    Maison Lumiere

    Maison Lumiere is a popular French restaurant offering classic French cuisine in a warm and elegant setting. The menu features timeless dishes such as beef bourguignon,coq au vin, creamy gratins, and delicate desserts made with traditionl techniques. Known for its refined flavors and cozy Parisian ambiance, Maison Lumiere is a favorite destination for those seeking an authentic French dining expereince.

    Trattoria Verona

    Trattoria Verona is a popular Italian dining spot known for its comforting, authentic flavors. The restaurant serves classic Italian dishes such as handmade pasta, wood-fired pizza, rich risottos, classic antipasi, and slow-cooked sauces made from fresh ingredients. With its warm atmosphere and rustic char, Trattoria Verona is a favorite place for relaxed dinners and family gatherings.
